About my background:

Though I've explored different academic paths in different places, I've been curious about studying CS since about 3rd grade; I learned what coding meant through an elementary code block game, and the creative possibility of it stuck with me since. I've lived in New Jersey my whole life, but I've spent time staying in Massachussets for high school, and at a liberal arts college in Pennsylvania for the beginning of undergrad. I'm half Ecuadorian, and have traveled to visit familiy multiple times since I was young! I flew on my own for the first time when I was 12, and I stayed for 3 months. Spending time with my (larger) extended family and exploring the gorgeous landscapes (from mountain ranges to mineral hot springs in the tropics) makes every visit incredibly memorable. More often though, I am traveling along the east coast (mainly Pennsylvania, Maryland, and New York) to spend time with my friends and family.

I've always been interested in varying creative outlets and have been lucky to explore them over the years.
